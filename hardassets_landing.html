FAQ
Common Questions
Is HardAssets.io really free?+
Yes. The core dashboard with all features — metals tracking, RE syndications, crypto, deal analyzer, and master portfolio — is completely free. No credit card required. We plan to offer premium features in the future, but the current product is free.
How is my data secured?+
Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with AES-256 encryption. Authentication is handled by Google Identity Services — we never see your password. All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
Do you connect to my bank or brokerage accounts?+
No. HardAssets.io is a manual-entry portfolio tracker. You add your holdings yourself or import from Excel/CSV. This means zero risk of unauthorized access to your financial accounts.
Can I import from a spreadsheet?+
Yes. Every tab supports Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV import. Upload your file, preview the data, and import in one click. The app recognizes common column names automatically.
What asset types can I track?+
Precious metals (gold, silver, platinum, palladium in 12 form types), real estate syndications (16 deal types), crypto (13+ coins), plus 11 asset classes in the master portfolio including equities, commodities, fixed income, private credit, alternatives, venture/PE, and cash.
Can I use it on my phone?+
Yes. HardAssets.io is fully responsive and works on any device with a web browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile. Your data syncs across all devices via your account.
