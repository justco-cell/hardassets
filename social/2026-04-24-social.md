3. **The math works.** Gold returned 50% year-over-year. Silver 131%. These aren't speculative rallies—they're structural responses to currency debasement, supply deficits, and geopolitical fragmentation.

**Target allocation:** 10-12% in precious metals (instead of the old 5%).

**Monitoring:** Track allocation against target monthly. Rebalance annually if metals appreciate beyond your target %.

This isn't market timing. It's portfolio insurance for a world where:
- Inflation won't die
- The dollar faces structural headwinds
- Central banks are rotating into gold
- Geopolitical risks are rising

Whether it's stagflation, currency crisis, or just garden-variety debasement, hard assets protect the portfolio that tech stocks won't.
